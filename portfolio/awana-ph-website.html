Awana PH Website

Awana PH is a non-profit Christian organization close to my heart. Established 1984 in the Philippines, it has been a strategic partner for churches and ministries to ensure the opportunity for all children and youth to be evangelized and discipled in Christ. To help improve their online presence, I was given the project of developing their website.

Building upon a responsive HTML5 template, I customized the site according to the organization's needs and specifications, working closely with their executive assistant. With the rise of smartphones, it is essential that sites are mobile-friendly, so proper implementations were made to fit this need.

The company's website already has a new version after they outsourced another developer to make it look more child-friendly, aligning better with the organization's mission and vision. As my very first freelance work, I enjoyed and learned so much with this project.
