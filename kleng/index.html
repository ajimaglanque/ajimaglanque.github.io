Heyyy. 😁 You know how I (hate) don't like goodbyes, right? 😂

But this isn't a goodbye. I refuse to call it one. We're stubborn like that. =)) This is just the digital version so you can access it anytime if you don't have the box with you. Just make sure you have internet though. :))

To say that I'm blessed meeting you in Awana would definitely be an understatement. I never expected the Lord to give me a best friend that I never knew I needed—not until you came. You've opened and touched parts of my heart that I didn't know existed, and you've helped me heal in more ways than you'll ever know.

I'm sorry that I can't come quickly and work with you fully in my favorite ministry. 🥺 But I know and I'm sure that we'll go beyond Awana, beyond the walls of Acacia, kase sure ako na 'yung friendship natin is one of the awesomest things God has ever created. 👌

You may be having a hard time owning some of what they have to say here about you, but the reality is that you have been a great blessing to many and no one can take that away from them and from you. You did well, Kleng. 🤗

It doesn't end here. In my eyes, you still shine as brightly as you did the first time that I've truly seen and known you. Keep on shining for Jesus by sharing His light to others. Every day I'm grateful for you at forever akong magiging proud sa'yo and of the fact na ikaw 'yung best friend ko. 😊
I love you and I'll always be here for you. 🤗

- G L E N G
